Gap vs Solution — Proposed Smart Attendance System

Shows the key limitations identified in previous research and how the proposed system addresses them.

Gap In Previous Research Papers Proposed System Solution
1. No Liveness Detection (prone to photo/video spoofing)
SolutionAdvanced Liveness Detection (anti-spoofing) combining depth estimation, motion/behavioral checks and CNN-based spoof classifiers to prevent photo, video and mask attacks.
2. Outdated Recognition Algorithms (Eigenfaces, LBPH) with limited robustness
SolutionModern Machine Learning models (FaceNet / ArcFace / ResNet-based embeddings) for robust, invariant face recognition under varied lighting, pose and occlusion.
3. Small Or Vague Datasets (poor generalization)
SolutionTrain / fine-tune on large, diverse datasets (e.g., VGGFace2, MS-Celeb or institutional data augmentation) and collect representative images at registration for better generalization.
4. No Evaluation Metrics Or Real-Time Benchmarks
SolutionProvide quantitative benchmarks — Accuracy, Precision, Recall, FAR/FRR and latency/FPS measurements for on-device and server scenarios.
5. Lack Of Data Security & Encryption
SolutionEncrypt stored embeddings and personal data (AES-256), secure transport (TLS), role-based access control and audit logging to protect privacy and integrity.
6. Limited Deployment & Reporting (Excel only)
SolutionCloud/ERP integration, real-time dashboards and automated email reporting (CSV/PDF exports) to support enterprise workflows and notifications.
